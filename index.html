Carrot Care Guide

Soil for Carrot

Loose, well-drained soil.

Carrots thrive in loose, well-drained soil with good aeration. Remove stones and debris to promote straight root growth.

Watering

Regular watering, avoiding overwatering.

Provide consistent moisture to carrot plants, but avoid overwatering, as it can lead to rotting and other issues.

Sunlight

Partial to full sun exposure.

Plant carrots in an area with partial to full sunlight for optimal growth and sweet flavor.

Benefits

Rich in vitamins and antioxidants.

Carrots are a rich source of vitamins and antioxidants, contributing to overall health and well-being.

Supports eye health.

Carrots are known for promoting good vision and supporting eye health due to their high beta-carotene content.

Considerations

Thin seedlings to prevent overcrowding; mulch to retain soil moisture.
